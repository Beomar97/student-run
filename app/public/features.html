About the Game

StudentRun is a competitive multiplayer game where you are able to compete against online opponents. The goal is to reach the finish line in the least amount of time as possible. Like other games in the "Jump 'n' Run" genre, the players will have to overcome obstacles and collect and use a wide range of items to gain an advantage against their opponents.
